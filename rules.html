COMMUNITY PRINCIPLES

Before joining The Stray Dogs RP, please take a moment to read our community principles. These guidelines are created to maintain a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for every member.


✦ Create a Safe and Positive Space

Stray Dogs is a place where everyone is welcome to express themselves freely in a positive, respectful, and responsible way. Let's make this community a place where everyone feels comfortable being themselves.


✦ Respect, Communicate, and Connect

Get to know one another, build friendships, and always communicate with kindness, respect, and good manners—regardless of your character or organization.


✦ Keep the Community Safe

Any NSFW, sexually suggestive, explicit, discriminatory, hateful, or otherwise inappropriate content is strictly prohibited. Let's keep this community safe and comfortable for everyone.


✦ Welcome Every Stray Dog

Support one another and warmly welcome new members. Help them feel that they've found a place where they belong. No one should feel left out.


✦ Participate Together

Community events are created for everyone to enjoy together. We encourage all members to participate whenever possible and help make every event a success.


✦ Respect Different Time Zones

Our members come from different countries and time zones. Please be patient, understanding, and respectful of each other's schedules and availability.


✦ No One Stands Alone

Never let anyone feel left out or alone. Reach out, start conversations, and support one another. Sometimes, a simple "hello" can make someone feel at home.

"No matter where we come from, every Stray Dog deserves a place to call home." 🥂
